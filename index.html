Motion Control PLC Programming Global Implementation

BAPTECO (Badr El-Din Petroleum Co.)

Our team's experience includes designing a comprehensive SCADA system to monitor and control two compressors, featuring real-time data visualization, efficient alarm management for over 1,000 alarms, and seamless communication via Modbus protocol, enhancing operational reliability.

System Upgrades Technical Consulting Tender Preparation

SIL 2 Safety Fire Safety Systems Emergency Response
